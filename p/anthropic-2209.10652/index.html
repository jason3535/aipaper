叠加的玩具模型

Toy Models of Superposition

摘要 · Abstract

神经网络常常将许多不相关的概念打包到单个神经元中——这种被称为“多义性”的令人费解的现象使得可解释性更具挑战性。本文提供了一个玩具模型，其中多义性可以被完全理解，它是模型以“叠加”方式存储额外稀疏特征的结果。我们证明了相变的存在、与均匀多面体几何的惊人联系，以及与对抗样本相关的证据。我们还讨论了对机制可解释性的潜在影响。

Neural networks often pack many unrelated concepts into a single neuron - a puzzling phenomenon known as 'polysemanticity' which makes interpretability much more challenging. This paper provides a toy model where polysemanticity can be fully understood, arising as a result of models storing additional sparse features in "superposition." We demonstrate the existence of a phase change, a surprising connection to the geometry of uniform polytopes, and evidence of a link to adversarial examples. We also discuss potential implications for mechanistic interpretability.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提供了一个玩具模型，其中多义性源于将稀疏特征存储在叠加态中。
    Provides a toy model where polysemanticity arises from storing sparse features in superposition.
  • 展示了模型行为随特征稀疏性变化的相变现象。
    Demonstrates a phase change in the model's behavior as feature sparsity varies.
  • 揭示了叠加态与均匀多胞体几何之间的惊人联系。
    Reveals a surprising connection between superposition and the geometry of uniform polytopes.
  • 提供了叠加态与对抗样本相关联的证据。
    Provides evidence linking superposition to adversarial examples.
  • 讨论了这对神经网络机制可解释性的启示。
    Discusses implications for mechanistic interpretability of neural networks.

局限 · Limitations

  • 玩具模型可能无法捕捉真实神经网络的所有复杂性。
    Toy model may not capture all complexities of real neural networks.
  • 结果基于受控稀疏性的合成数据。
    Results are based on synthetic data with controlled sparsity.
  • 相变和多胞体联系可能无法推广到其他架构。
    Phase change and polytope connections may not generalize to other architectures.
  • 对抗样本的联系是初步的，需要进一步验证。
    Adversarial example link is preliminary and requires further validation.
