Adam: 一种随机优化方法

Adam: A Method for Stochastic Optimization

摘要 · Abstract

我们介绍了 Adam，一种基于低阶矩自适应估计的随机目标函数一阶梯度优化算法。该方法易于实现，计算效率高，内存需求小，对梯度的对角缩放具有不变性，非常适合数据和/或参数规模较大的问题。该方法也适用于非平稳目标以及噪声和/或梯度非常稀疏的问题。超参数具有直观的解释，通常只需很少的调整。讨论了与启发 Adam 的相关算法的一些联系。我们还分析了该算法的理论收敛性质，并提供了与在线凸优化框架下已知最佳结果相当的收敛速度遗憾界。实证结果表明，Adam 在实践中表现良好，并优于其他随机优化方法。最后，我们讨论了 AdaMax，一种基于无穷范数的 Adam 变体。

We introduce Adam, an algorithm for first-order gradient-based optimization of stochastic objective functions, based on adaptive estimates of lower-order moments. The method is straightforward to implement, is computationally efficient, has little memory requirements, is invariant to diagonal rescaling of the gradients, and is well suited for problems that are large in terms of data and/or parameters. The method is also appropriate for non-stationary objectives and problems with very noisy and/or sparse gradients. The hyper-parameters have intuitive interpretations and typically require little tuning. Some connections to related algorithms, on which Adam was inspired, are discussed. We also analyze the theoretical convergence properties of the algorithm and provide a regret bound on the convergence rate that is comparable to the best known results under the online convex optimization framework. Empirical results demonstrate that Adam works well in practice and compares favorably to other stochastic optimization methods. Finally, we discuss AdaMax, a variant of Adam based on the infinity norm.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出了Adam，一种高效的一阶梯度优化算法。
    Introduces Adam, an efficient first-order gradient-based optimization algorithm.
  • 使用低阶矩的自适应估计进行参数更新。
    Uses adaptive estimates of lower-order moments for parameter updates.
  • 对梯度的对角缩放具有不变性，适用于大规模问题。
    Invariant to diagonal rescaling of gradients, suitable for large-scale problems.
  • 有效处理非平稳目标和噪声/稀疏梯度。
    Handles non-stationary objectives and noisy/sparse gradients effectively.
  • 提供了理论收敛性分析，遗憾界与已知最佳结果相当。
    Provides theoretical convergence analysis with a regret bound comparable to best known results.
  • 在多种任务上实证优于其他随机优化方法。
    Empirically outperforms other stochastic optimization methods on various tasks.

局限 · Limitations

  • 收敛性分析假设凸目标，非凸问题不保证。
    Convergence analysis assumes convex objectives, not guaranteed for non-convex problems.
  • 超参数虽直观，但特定应用仍需调参。
    Hyper-parameters may require tuning for specific applications despite intuitive interpretation.
  • 由于存储矩，内存需求随参数数量线性增长。
    Memory requirements scale with number of parameters due to moment storage.
  • 在极高维或病态问题上性能可能下降。
    Performance may degrade on very high-dimensional or ill-conditioned problems.
