智能爆炸的不可能性

The impossibility of intelligence explosion

弗朗索瓦·肖莱 Francois Chollet · Google · 2017-11-27 · Chollet Essay ↗

摘要 · Abstract

1. 一种源于对智能误解的有缺陷的推理。3. 我们的环境对个体智能设置了硬性限制。4. 我们的大部分智能并不在大脑中，而是外化为我们的文明。

1. A flawed reasoning that stems from a misunderstanding of intelligence 3. Our environment puts a hard limit on our individual intelligence 4. Most of our intelligence is not in our brain, it is externalized as our civilization

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 智能是情境性的，不能脱离其环境、身体和文化。
    Intelligence is situational and cannot be separated from its environment, body, and culture.
  • 没有单个大脑能设计出比自己更聪明的智能，数十亿人脑的历史证明了这一点。
    No individual brain can design a greater intelligence than itself, as evidenced by billions of human brains.
  • 人类智能大部分外化于文明中，而非个体大脑内。
    Most human intelligence is externalized in civilization, not in individual brains.
  • 递归自我改进系统在实践中呈现线性或S形进步，而非指数爆炸。
    Recursively self-improving systems in practice show linear or sigmoidal progress, not exponential explosion.
  • 科学进步作为递归AI改进的类似物，尽管投入指数级努力，进步却是线性的。
    Scientific progress, a close analog to recursive AI improvement, has been linear despite exponential effort.
  • 智能爆炸叙事源于对智能和递归系统的误解。
    The intelligence explosion narrative is based on a misunderstanding of intelligence and recursive systems.

局限 · Limitations

  • 论证依赖人类历史的经验观察，可能不适用于数字AI。
    The argument relies on empirical observations from human history, which may not apply to digital AI.
  • 引用‘没有免费午餐’定理过于抽象；现实AI可能利用特定问题的结构。
    The 'no free lunch' theorem is cited abstractly; real-world AI may exploit structure in specific problems.
  • 如果AI能快速重塑环境，环境是瓶颈的说法可能受到挑战。
    The claim that environment is the bottleneck may be challenged if AI can rapidly reshape its environment.
  • 分析假设AI将受人类类似约束，这对数字心智可能不成立。
    The analysis assumes that AI will be embedded in human-like constraints, which may not hold for digital minds.
  • 科学的线性进步可能源于人类局限；AI可能克服这些瓶颈。
    The linear progress of science could be due to human limitations; AI might overcome these bottlenecks.
