Transformers are SSMs: Generalized Models and Efficient Algorithms Through Structured State Space Duality

特里·道 Tri Dao · Princeton University · 2024-05-31 · arXiv:2405.21060 ↗

摘要 · Abstract

尽管Transformer一直是深度学习在语言建模中成功的主要架构，但最近诸如Mamba之类的状态空间模型（SSM）已显示出在中小规模上能够匹配或超越Transformer。我们证明这些模型家族实际上密切相关，并通过一个研究充分的结构化半可分离矩阵类别的各种分解，建立了SSM与注意力变体之间的丰富理论联系框架。我们的状态空间对偶性（SSD）框架使我们能够设计一种新架构（Mamba-2），其核心层是对Mamba选择性SSM的改进，速度提升2-8倍，同时在语言建模方面继续与Transformer竞争。

While Transformers have been the main architecture behind deep learning's success in language modeling, state-space models (SSMs) such as Mamba have recently been shown to match or outperform Transformers at small to medium scale. We show that these families of models are actually quite closely related, and develop a rich framework of theoretical connections between SSMs and variants of attention, connected through various decompositions of a well-studied class of structured semiseparable matrices. Our state space duality (SSD) framework allows us to design a new architecture (Mamba-2) whose core layer is an a refinement of Mamba's selective SSM that is 2-8X faster, while continuing to be competitive with Transformers on language modeling.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 证明Transformer和SSM通过结构化半可分离矩阵紧密相关。
    Shows that Transformers and SSMs are closely related through structured semiseparable matrices.
  • 开发了连接SSM和注意力变体的理论框架（SSD）。
    Develops a theoretical framework (SSD) connecting SSMs and attention variants.
  • 设计了Mamba-2，对Mamba的选择性SSM进行了更快的改进。
    Designs Mamba-2, a faster refinement of Mamba's selective SSM.
  • 在保持竞争力的语言建模性能的同时，比Mamba提速2-8倍。
    Achieves 2-8X speedup over Mamba while maintaining competitive language modeling.
  • 在统一的数学视角下统一了两大模型家族。
    Unifies two major model families under a common mathematical lens.

局限 · Limitations

  • 实证验证仅限于中小规模模型。
    Empirical validation limited to small to medium scale models.
  • 加速效果可能不适用于所有硬件或序列长度。
    Speedup claims may not hold for all hardware or sequence lengths.
  • 理论联系可能不适用于所有SSM或注意力变体。
    Theoretical connections may not extend to all SSM or attention variants.
  • 未在语言建模之外的下游任务上进行评估。
    No evaluation on downstream tasks beyond language modeling.
