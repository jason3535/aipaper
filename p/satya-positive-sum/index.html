正和未来

Positive Sum Future

萨提亚·纳德拉 Satya Nadella · Microsoft · 2025-11-15 · Satya Nadella Essay ↗

摘要 · Abstract

关于技术进步和实际影响的笔记。我一直在思考AI平台浪潮的净收益是什么。真正的问题是如何赋能每一家公司，让他们从这一平台转变中获得更多，并构建自己的人工智能原生能力和企业价值（而不是无意中将其独特价值转移给科技行业！！）。比尔曾有名言：平台是指所有使用它的人的经济价值超过创建它的公司的价值。这就是正和未来的本质。

Notes on advances in technology and real-world impact I’ve been thinking a lot about what the net benefit of the AI platform wave is. The real question is how to empower every company out there to get more out of this platform shift and build their own AI native capabilities and enterprise value (vs inadvertently just transfer their unique value to the tech sector!!). Bill famously said a platform is when the economic value of everybody that uses it exceeds the value of the company that creates it. That’s the essence of the positive-sum future.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出AI平台浪潮的正和框架，用户价值超过创造者价值。
    Proposes a positive-sum framework for AI platform waves, where user value exceeds creator value.
  • 强调与OpenAI的合作是互惠价值创造和规模扩张的典范。
    Highlights partnership with OpenAI as a model for mutual value creation and scaling.
  • 描述了与OpenAI和Nvidia共同设计全球首个AI超级工厂，用于前沿模型训练。
    Describes co-design of world's first AI superfactory with OpenAI and Nvidia for frontier model training.
  • 指出GitHub Copilot等AI编码工具扩展，重新定义软件类别。
    Notes expansion of AI coding tools like GitHub Copilot, redefining software categories.
  • 强调超越科技领域的广泛经济和社会效益，如制药、制造、教育。
    Emphasizes broad economic and societal benefits beyond tech sector, e.g., pharma, manufacturing, education.
  • 呼吁分布式控制与主权，避免科技巨头榨取价值。
    Calls for distributed control and sovereignty to avoid value extraction by tech giants.

局限 · Limitations

  • 正和结果依赖于公平的合作伙伴关系，这并非总能实现。
    Positive-sum outcomes depend on equitable partnerships, which may not always be achievable.
  • 框架假设AI效益广泛扩散，但实际采用可能不均衡。
    Framework assumes broad diffusion of AI benefits, but real-world adoption may be uneven.
  • 关注平台经济学可能忽视就业替代等负面外部性。
    Focus on platform economics may overlook negative externalities like job displacement.
  • 例子（如制药、制造）是理想化的，缺乏具体影响证据。
    Examples (e.g., pharma, manufacturing) are aspirational and lack concrete evidence of impact.
  • 行业的零和思维可能阻碍正和伙伴关系的实现。
    Zero-sum mindset in industry may hinder realization of positive-sum partnerships.
