理解深度学习需要重新思考泛化

Understanding deep learning requires rethinking generalization

奥里奥尔·维尼亚尔斯 Oriol Vinyals · Google · 2016-11-10 · arXiv:1611.03530 ↗

摘要 · Abstract

尽管深度人工神经网络规模庞大，但其训练与测试性能之间的差异却可以非常小。传统观点认为，小的泛化误差要么归因于模型族的性质，要么归因于训练中使用的正则化技术。通过广泛的系统实验，我们展示了这些传统方法为何无法解释大型神经网络在实践中良好泛化的原因。具体而言，我们的实验证实，使用随机梯度方法训练的最先进的图像分类卷积网络，能够轻松拟合训练数据的随机标签。这一现象在定性上不受显式正则化的影响，甚至当我们用完全无结构的随机噪声替换真实图像时也会发生。我们通过理论构建佐证了这些实验发现，表明简单的两层神经网络在参数数量超过数据点数量时（这在实践中很常见），已经具有完美的有限样本表达能力。我们通过与传统模型的比较来解释我们的实验发现。

Despite their massive size, successful deep artificial neural networks can exhibit a remarkably small difference between training and test performance. Conventional wisdom attributes small generalization error either to properties of the model family, or to the regularization techniques used during training. Through extensive systematic experiments, we show how these traditional approaches fail to explain why large neural networks generalize well in practice. Specifically, our experiments establish that state-of-the-art convolutional networks for image classification trained with stochastic gradient methods easily fit a random labeling of the training data. This phenomenon is qualitatively unaffected by explicit regularization, and occurs even if we replace the true images by completely unstructured random noise. We corroborate these experimental findings with a theoretical construction showing that simple depth two neural networks already have perfect finite sample expressivity as soon as the number of parameters exceeds the number of data points as it usually does in practice. We interpret our experimental findings by comparison with traditional models.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 展示深度网络能拟合随机标签，挑战传统泛化理论。
    Shows that deep networks can fit random labels, challenging traditional generalization theory.
  • 证明显式正则化在定性上不影响拟合随机标签。
    Demonstrates that explicit regularization does not qualitatively affect fitting random labels.
  • 发现网络拟合无结构随机噪声与真实图像一样容易。
    Finds that networks fit unstructured random noise as easily as true images.
  • 提供理论构造，表明当参数超过数据点时，深度为2的网络具有完美的有限样本表达能力。
    Provides theoretical construction showing depth-2 networks have perfect finite sample expressivity when parameters exceed data points.
  • 通过与核方法等传统模型比较来解释发现。
    Interprets findings by comparing with traditional models like kernel methods.

局限 · Limitations

  • 实验聚焦于卷积网络的图像分类，未涉及其他架构。
    Experiments focus on image classification with convolutional networks, not other architectures.
  • 理论构造限于深度为2的网络，未涉及更深架构。
    Theoretical construction limited to depth-2 networks, not deeper architectures.
  • 研究未解释为何网络在拟合噪声后仍能在真实数据上良好泛化。
    Study does not address why networks generalize well on real data despite fitting noise.
  • 结果可能不适用于自然语言处理等其他任务。
    Results may not extend to other tasks like natural language processing.
