Gemini：一个高度能力多模态模型家族

Gemini: A Family of Highly Capable Multimodal Models

诺姆·沙泽尔 Noam Shazeer · Google DeepMind · 2023-12-19 · arXiv:2312.11805 ↗

摘要 · Abstract

本报告介绍了一个新的多模态模型家族 Gemini，它在图像、音频、视频和文本理解方面展现出卓越的能力。Gemini 家族包括 Ultra、Pro 和 Nano 三种尺寸，适用于从复杂推理任务到设备上内存受限场景的各种应用。在广泛的基准测试评估中，我们最强大的 Gemini Ultra 模型在32个基准测试中的30个上取得了最先进的结果——值得注意的是，它是第一个在广受研究的考试基准 MMLU 上达到人类专家水平的模型，并在我们考察的20个多模态基准测试中均提升了最先进水平。我们相信，Gemini 家族在跨模态推理和语言理解方面的新能力将支持多种用例。我们讨论了通过 Gemini、Gemini Advanced、Google AI Studio 和 Cloud Vertex AI 等服务负责任地对 Gemini 模型进行后训练和部署的方法。

This report introduces a new family of multimodal models, Gemini, that exhibit remarkable capabilities across image, audio, video, and text understanding. The Gemini family consists of Ultra, Pro, and Nano sizes, suitable for applications ranging from complex reasoning tasks to on-device memory-constrained use-cases. Evaluation on a broad range of benchmarks shows that our most-capable Gemini Ultra model advances the state of the art in 30 of 32 of these benchmarks - notably being the first model to achieve human-expert performance on the well-studied exam benchmark MMLU, and improving the state of the art in every one of the 20 multimodal benchmarks we examined. We believe that the new capabilities of the Gemini family in cross-modal reasoning and language understanding will enable a wide variety of use cases. We discuss our approach toward post-training and deploying Gemini models responsibly to users through services including Gemini, Gemini Advanced, Google AI Studio, and Cloud Vertex AI.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出Gemini系列（Ultra、Pro、Nano），实现跨图像、音频、视频和文本的多模态理解。
    Introduces Gemini family (Ultra, Pro, Nano) for multimodal understanding across image, audio, video, and text.
  • Gemini Ultra在32项基准测试中的30项上达到最先进水平，首个在MMLU上超越人类专家。
    Gemini Ultra achieves state-of-the-art on 30 of 32 benchmarks, first to surpass human-expert on MMLU.
  • 在所有评估的20个多模态基准测试上均提升最先进水平。
    Advances state-of-the-art on all 20 multimodal benchmarks evaluated.
  • 展示了强大的跨模态推理和语言理解能力。
    Demonstrates strong cross-modal reasoning and language understanding capabilities.
  • 提供三种模型尺寸，适用于从复杂推理到设备端部署等多种场景。
    Provides three model sizes for diverse deployment scenarios from complex reasoning to on-device.
  • 讨论了通过Gemini、Gemini Advanced、AI Studio和Vertex AI进行负责任的训练后处理和部署。
    Discusses responsible post-training and deployment via Gemini, Gemini Advanced, AI Studio, and Vertex AI.

局限 · Limitations

  • 仅限于以英语为中心的基准测试；未广泛评估多语言性能。
    Limited to English-centric benchmarks; multilingual performance not extensively evaluated.
  • 在需要实时或低延迟推理的任务上，模型能力可能下降。
    Model capabilities may degrade on tasks requiring real-time or low-latency inference.
  • 训练数据中的潜在偏见未完全解决；公平性和安全性需要进一步研究。
    Potential biases from training data not fully addressed; fairness and safety require further study.
  • Ultra模型的计算成本高，限制了小型组织的可及性。
    Computational cost for Ultra model is high, limiting accessibility for smaller organizations.
  • 跨模态推理可能在高度专业化或分布外输入上失败。
    Cross-modal reasoning may fail on highly specialized or out-of-distribution inputs.
