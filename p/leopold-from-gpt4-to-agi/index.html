第一部分：从 GPT-4 到 AGI：计算数量级提升。第二部分：从 AGI 到超级智能：智能爆炸。第三部分a：竞逐万亿美元集群。

* I. From GPT-4 to AGI: Counting the OOMs * II. From AGI to Superintelligence: the Intelligence Explosion * IIIa. Racing to the Trillion-Dollar Cluster

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 追踪算力、算法效率和去束缚增益，预测到2027年将出现另一个GPT-2到GPT-4级别的跃升。
    Traces compute, algorithmic efficiency, and unhobbling gains to project another GPT-2-to-GPT-4 jump by 2027.
  • 认为通过外推趋势线，到2027年实现AGI是合理的，无需科幻信仰。
    Argues AGI by 2027 is plausible via extrapolating trendlines, not requiring sci-fi beliefs.
  • 识别出去束缚（RLHF、思维链、工具、脚手架）是解锁潜在能力的关键。
    Identifies unhobbling (RLHF, chain-of-thought, tools, scaffolding) as key to unlocking latent capabilities.
  • 强调测试时算力过剩是未来能力提升的主要来源。
    Highlights test-time compute overhang as a major source of future capability gains.
  • 预测到2027年将从聊天机器人过渡到可替代远程工人的智能体。
    Predicts transition from chatbots to drop-in remote worker agents by 2027.
  • 声称数据墙可能通过合成数据、自我对弈和强化学习克服，维持缩放趋势。
    Claims data wall may be overcome via synthetic data, self-play, and RL, maintaining scaling trends.

局限 · Limitations

  • 依赖趋势线外推，可能因数据墙或算法限制而失效。
    Relies on extrapolation of trendlines that may break due to data wall or algorithmic limits.
  • 去束缚增益可能有限；在摘取低垂果实后，进一步改进可能减少。
    Unhobbling gains may be finite; further improvements could diminish after low-hanging fruit.
  • 由于专有研究和缺乏公开数据，算法效率的估计存在不确定性。
    Estimates of algorithmic efficiency are uncertain due to proprietary research and lack of public data.
  • 即使技术上实现AGI，社会推广和监管也可能减缓采用。
    Societal rollout and regulation could slow adoption even if technical AGI is achieved.
  • 聚焦认知任务；机器人和物理世界交互可能滞后。
    Focuses on cognitive tasks; robotics and physical world interaction may lag behind.
