改进Wasserstein GAN的训练方法

Improved Training of Wasserstein GANs

马丁·阿尔约夫斯基 Martin Arjovsky · MILA / Université de Montréal · 2017-03-31 · arXiv:1704.00028 ↗ · 被引 10956

摘要 · Abstract

生成对抗网络（GAN）是强大的生成模型，但存在训练不稳定的问题。最近提出的Wasserstein GAN（WGAN）在稳定GAN训练方面取得了进展，但有时仍只能生成低质量样本或无法收敛。我们发现这些问题通常是由于WGAN中使用权重裁剪来对判别器施加Lipschitz约束，这可能导致不良行为。我们提出了一种替代裁剪权重的方法：惩罚判别器相对于其输入的梯度范数。我们提出的方法优于标准WGAN，并且能够在几乎不需要超参数调优的情况下稳定训练各种GAN架构，包括101层残差网络和离散数据的语言模型。我们还在CIFAR-10和LSUN卧室数据集上实现了高质量生成。

Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) are powerful generative models, but suffer from training instability. The recently proposed Wasserstein GAN (WGAN) makes progress toward stable training of GANs, but sometimes can still generate only low-quality samples or fail to converge. We find that these problems are often due to the use of weight clipping in WGAN to enforce a Lipschitz constraint on the critic, which can lead to undesired behavior. We propose an alternative to clipping weights: penalize the norm of gradient of the critic with respect to its input. Our proposed method performs better than standard WGAN and enables stable training of a wide variety of GAN architectures with almost no hyperparameter tuning, including 101-layer ResNets and language models over discrete data. We also achieve high quality generations on CIFAR-10 and LSUN bedrooms.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 指出WGAN中的权重裁剪会导致不良行为和训练不稳定。
    Identifies that weight clipping in WGAN causes undesired behavior and training instability.
  • 提出梯度惩罚作为权重裁剪的替代方案，以强制执行Lipschitz约束。
    Proposes gradient penalty as an alternative to weight clipping for enforcing Lipschitz constraint.
  • 实现了多种GAN架构的稳定训练，几乎无需超参数调整。
    Enables stable training of diverse GAN architectures with minimal hyperparameter tuning.
  • 在101层ResNet和离散数据语言模型上展示了有效性。
    Demonstrates effectiveness on 101-layer ResNets and language models over discrete data.
  • 在CIFAR-10和LSUN卧室数据集上生成了高质量样本。
    Achieves high-quality generations on CIFAR-10 and LSUN bedrooms.

局限 · Limitations

  • 与权重裁剪相比，梯度惩罚增加了计算开销。
    Gradient penalty adds computational overhead compared to weight clipping.
  • 该方法可能无法推广到所有GAN变体或训练设置。
    Method may not generalize to all GAN variants or training settings.
  • 评估仅限于图像和离散数据领域。
    Evaluation limited to image and discrete data domains.
  • 需要仔细调整惩罚系数λ。
    Requires careful tuning of penalty coefficient λ.
