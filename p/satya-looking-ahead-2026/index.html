摘要 · Abstract

关于技术进步及其现实影响的笔记

Notes on advances in technology and real-world impact

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出AI应作为人类潜能的支架，而非替代品。
    Proposes AI as scaffolding for human potential, not a substitute.
  • 强调从模型向系统演进，协调多个智能体和工具。
    Emphasizes evolution from models to systems with orchestrated agents and tools.
  • 强调需审慎推广AI以应对社会挑战。
    Stresses need for deliberate diffusion of AI to address societal challenges.
  • 强调AI获得社会许可需有现实世界评估影响。
    Highlights real-world eval impact for societal permission of AI.
  • 呼吁建立包含认知放大器的新心智理论平衡。
    Calls for new equilibrium in theory of mind with cognitive amplifiers.
  • 指出2026年是AI从发现走向广泛扩散的关键年。
    Notes 2026 as pivotal year for AI diffusion beyond initial discovery.

局限 · Limitations

  • 缺乏如何构建AI支架的具体技术细节。
    Lacks specific technical details on how to build AI scaffolds.
  • 未讨论推广可能带来的负面社会影响。
    Does not address potential negative societal impacts of diffusion.
  • 假设资源分配共识可实现，但未考虑现实冲突。
    Assumes consensus on resource allocation is achievable.
  • 对如何衡量现实世界评估影响的讨论有限。
    Limited discussion on how to measure real-world eval impact.
  • 侧重乐观视角，忽略滥用或不平等风险。
    Focuses on optimistic view, ignoring risks of misuse or inequality.
