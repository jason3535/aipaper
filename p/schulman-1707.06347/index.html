近端策略优化算法

Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms

约翰·舒尔曼 John Schulman · OpenAI · 2017-07-20 · arXiv:1707.06347 ↗ · 被引 28997

摘要 · Abstract

我们提出了一类新的用于强化学习的策略梯度方法，这些方法在与环境交互采样数据和利用随机梯度上升优化“替代”目标函数之间交替进行。标准策略梯度方法每个数据样本只进行一次梯度更新，而我们提出了一个新的目标函数，使得可以进行多个小批量更新周期。这种新方法被称为近端策略优化（PPO），它兼具信任区域策略优化（TRPO）的一些优点，但实现更简单、更通用，并且经验上样本复杂度更优。我们的实验在包括模拟机器人运动和Atari游戏在内的基准任务上测试了PPO，结果表明PPO优于其他在线策略梯度方法，并且在样本复杂度、简单性和实际运行时间之间取得了良好的平衡。

We propose a new family of policy gradient methods for reinforcement learning, which alternate between sampling data through interaction with the environment, and optimizing a "surrogate" objective function using stochastic gradient ascent. Whereas standard policy gradient methods perform one gradient update per data sample, we propose a novel objective function that enables multiple epochs of minibatch updates. The new methods, which we call proximal policy optimization (PPO), have some of the benefits of trust region policy optimization (TRPO), but they are much simpler to implement, more general, and have better sample complexity (empirically). Our experiments test PPO on a collection of benchmark tasks, including simulated robotic locomotion and Atari game playing, and we show that PPO outperforms other online policy gradient methods, and overall strikes a favorable balance between sample complexity, simplicity, and wall-time.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出PPO，一类使用裁剪替代目标进行稳定更新的策略梯度方法。
    Proposes PPO, a family of policy gradient methods using a clipped surrogate objective for stable updates.
  • 每个数据样本支持多个小批量更新周期，提高样本效率。
    Enables multiple epochs of minibatch updates per data sample, improving sample efficiency.
  • 比TRPO更易实现，同时保留相似的性能优势。
    Simpler to implement than TRPO while retaining similar performance benefits.
  • 在机器人运动和Atari游戏上经验性地优于其他在线策略梯度方法。
    Empirically outperforms other online policy gradient methods on robotic locomotion and Atari games.
  • 在样本复杂度、简单性和运行时间之间取得良好平衡。
    Strikes a favorable balance between sample complexity, simplicity, and wall-time.

局限 · Limitations

  • PPO的裁剪目标在某些情况下仍可能允许较大的策略更新。
    PPO's clipped objective may still allow large policy updates in some cases.
  • 需要仔细调整超参数，如裁剪阈值和学习率。
    Requires careful tuning of hyperparameters like clip epsilon and learning rate.
  • 在稀疏奖励或长视野任务上的性能可能下降。
    Performance on tasks with sparse rewards or long horizons may degrade.
  • 理论收敛保证尚未完全建立。
    Theoretical convergence guarantees are not fully established.
  • 可能无法很好地扩展到非常高维的动作空间。
    May not scale well to very high-dimensional action spaces.
