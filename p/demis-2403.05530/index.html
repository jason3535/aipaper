Gemini 1.5：解锁跨越数百万词元的多模态理解

Gemini 1.5: Unlocking multimodal understanding across millions of tokens of context

摘要 · Abstract

本报告介绍了 Gemini 1.5 系列模型，这是下一代高度计算高效的多模态模型，能够从数百万词元的上下文中回忆和推理细粒度信息，包括多个长文档以及数小时的视频和音频。该系列包括两个新模型：(1) 更新的 Gemini 1.5 Pro，在绝大多数能力和基准测试上超越了二月份版本；(2) Gemini 1.5 Flash，一种更轻量级的变体，旨在提高效率且质量下降极小。Gemini 1.5 模型在跨模态的长上下文检索任务上实现了近乎完美的召回，改进了长文档问答、长视频问答和长上下文 ASR 的最新技术水平，并在广泛的基准测试中匹配或超越了 Gemini 1.0 Ultra 的最优性能。通过研究 Gemini 1.5 长上下文能力的极限，我们发现其在至少 1000 万词元上持续改进下一个词元预测和近乎完美的召回（>99%），这是对现有模型（如 Claude 3.0 的 20 万词元和 GPT-4 Turbo 的 12.8 万词元）的代际飞跃。最后，我们强调了实际应用案例，例如 Gemini 1.5 与专业人士协作完成任务，在 10 个不同工作类别中实现了 26% 到 75% 的时间节省，以及前沿大语言模型令人惊讶的新能力：当给定一种全球使用人数不足 200 的 Kalamang 语言的语法手册时，该模型能够学习将英语翻译成 Kalamang，其水平与从相同内容学习的人类相当。

In this report, we introduce the Gemini 1.5 family of models, representing the next generation of highly compute-efficient multimodal models capable of recalling and reasoning over fine-grained information from millions of tokens of context, including multiple long documents and hours of video and audio. The family includes two new models: (1) an updated Gemini 1.5 Pro, which exceeds the February version on the great majority of capabilities and benchmarks; (2) Gemini 1.5 Flash, a more lightweight variant designed for efficiency with minimal regression in quality. Gemini 1.5 models achieve near-perfect recall on long-context retrieval tasks across modalities, improve the state-of-the-art in long-document QA, long-video QA and long-context ASR, and match or surpass Gemini 1.0 Ultra's state-of-the-art performance across a broad set of benchmarks. Studying the limits of Gemini 1.5's long-context ability, we find continued improvement in next-token prediction and near-perfect retrieval (&gt;99%) up to at least 10M tokens, a generational leap over existing models such as Claude 3.0 (200k) and GPT-4 Turbo (128k). Finally, we highlight real-world use cases, such as Gemini 1.5 collaborating with professionals on completing their tasks achieving 26 to 75% time savings across 10 different job categories, as well as surprising new capabilities of large language models at the frontier; when given a grammar manual for Kalamang, a language with fewer than 200 speakers worldwide, the model learns to translate English to Kalamang at a similar level to a person who learned from the same content.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 推出 Gemini 1.5 Pro 和 Flash 模型，支持高达 1000 万 token 的长上下文理解。
    Introduces Gemini 1.5 Pro and Flash models with long-context understanding up to 10M tokens.
  • 在文本、视频和音频的长上下文检索任务中实现近乎完美的召回率（>99%）。
    Achieves near-perfect recall (>99%) on long-context retrieval tasks across text, video, and audio.
  • 在长文档问答、长视频问答和长上下文语音识别中树立新标杆。
    Sets new state-of-the-art in long-document QA, long-video QA, and long-context ASR.
  • 在广泛基准测试中匹配或超越 Gemini 1.0 Ultra 的性能。
    Matches or surpasses Gemini 1.0 Ultra across a broad set of benchmarks.
  • 在 10 个职业类别中通过专业协作实现 26-75% 的时间节省。
    Demonstrates 26-75% time savings across 10 job categories via professional collaboration.
  • 从语法手册学习英语到卡拉芒语的翻译，达到人类水平的学习效果。
    Learns to translate English to Kalamang from a grammar manual, matching human-level learning.

局限 · Limitations

  • 长上下文性能仅评估至 1000 万 token，超出可能下降。
    Long-context performance evaluated only up to 10M tokens; beyond may degrade.
  • 实际时间节省基于特定专业任务，泛化性未验证。
    Real-world time savings based on specific professional tasks; generalizability unverified.
  • 卡拉芒语翻译能力仅针对单一低资源语言展示，未泛化。
    Kalamang translation capability demonstrated for a single low-resource language; not generalized.
  • Pro 和 Flash 变体之间的效率权衡未完全表征。
    Model efficiency trade-offs between Pro and Flash variants not fully characterized.
  • 基准比较限于 Gemini 1.0 Ultra、Claude 3.0 和 GPT-4 Turbo，需更广泛对比。
    Benchmark comparisons limited to Gemini 1.0 Ultra, Claude 3.0, and GPT-4 Turbo; broader comparison needed.
