Aya Model: An Instruction Finetuned Open-Access Multilingual Language Model

摘要 · Abstract

近期大型语言模型的突破主要集中在少数数据丰富的语言上。如何将突破性成果扩展到这些“第一梯队”语言之外？我们的工作介绍了Aya，一个大规模多语言生成式语言模型，能够遵循101种语言的指令，其中超过50%被视为低资源语言。Aya在大多数任务上优于mT0和BLOOMZ，同时覆盖的语言数量是它们的两倍。我们引入了广泛的评估套件，将多语言评估的前沿扩展到99种语言——包括判别式和生成式任务、人工评估以及模拟胜率，覆盖了保留任务和分布内性能。此外，我们对最优微调混合组成、数据剪枝以及模型的毒性、偏见和安全性进行了详细调查。我们在https://hf.co/CohereForAI/aya-101开源了我们的指令数据集和模型。

Recent breakthroughs in large language models (LLMs) have centered around a handful of data-rich languages. What does it take to broaden access to breakthroughs beyond first-class citizen languages? Our work introduces Aya, a massively multilingual generative language model that follows instructions in 101 languages of which over 50% are considered as lower-resourced. Aya outperforms mT0 and BLOOMZ on the majority of tasks while covering double the number of languages. We introduce extensive new evaluation suites that broaden the state-of-art for multilingual eval across 99 languages -- including discriminative and generative tasks, human evaluation, and simulated win rates that cover both held-out tasks and in-distribution performance. Furthermore, we conduct detailed investigations on the optimal finetuning mixture composition, data pruning, as well as the toxicity, bias, and safety of our models. We open-source our instruction datasets and our model at https://hf.co/CohereForAI/aya-101

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出 Aya，一个覆盖 101 种语言（其中超过 50% 为低资源语言）的大规模多语言指令微调模型。
    Introduces Aya, a massively multilingual instruction-finetuned model covering 101 languages, over 50% lower-resourced.
  • 在覆盖两倍语言数量的同时，在大多数任务上优于 mT0 和 BLOOMZ。
    Outperforms mT0 and BLOOMZ on most tasks while covering twice as many languages.
  • 提供覆盖 99 种语言的广泛评估套件，包括判别式、生成式、人工评估和模拟胜率。
    Provides extensive evaluation suites for 99 languages, including discriminative, generative, human eval, and simulated win rates.
  • 研究了最优微调混合组成和数据剪枝策略。
    Investigates optimal finetuning mixture composition and data pruning strategies.
  • 分析了模型的毒性、偏见和安全性。
    Analyzes toxicity, bias, and safety of the model.
  • 开源指令数据集和模型，以促进多语言研究。
    Open-sources instruction datasets and the model to facilitate multilingual research.

局限 · Limitations

  • 由于资源限制，评估可能未均匀覆盖所有 101 种语言。
    Evaluation may not cover all 101 languages equally due to resource constraints.
  • 模型在极低资源语言上的性能可能有限。
    Model performance on extremely low-resource languages may be limited.
  • 安全性分析是初步的，可能未涵盖所有边缘情况。
    Safety analysis is preliminary and may not capture all edge cases.
  • 研究聚焦于指令遵循，其他能力如推理未深入探索。
    The study focuses on instruction following; other capabilities like reasoning are not deeply explored.
  • 开源数据集可能包含来自网络爬取数据的偏见。
    Open-source datasets may contain biases from web-crawled data.
