摘要 · Abstract

达里奥·阿莫代，Anthropic公司的CEO，经常谈论强大AI的风险，但这并不意味着他是悲观主义者。他认为大多数人低估了AI的积极潜力，正如他们低估了风险一样。

1https://allpoetry.com/All-Watched-Over-By-Machines-Of-Loving-Grace How AI Could Transform the World for the Better I think and talk a lot about the risks of powerful AI. The company I’m the CEO of, Anthropic, does a lot of research on how to reduce these risks. Because of this, people sometimes draw the conclusion that I’m a pessimist or “doomer” who thinks AI will be mostly bad or dangerous. I don’t think that at all. In fact, one of my main reasons for focusing on risks is that they’re the only thing standing between us and what I see as a fundamentally positive future. I think that most people are underestimating just how radical the upside of AI could be, just as I think most people are underestimating how bad the risks could be.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 强大的人工智能可将50-100年的生物医学进步压缩到5-10年内，治愈大多数疾病并将人类寿命延长一倍。
    Powerful AI could compress 50-100 years of biological and medical progress into 5-10 years, curing most diseases and doubling human lifespan.
  • 人工智能加速的神经科学可治愈大多数精神疾病，并大幅提升认知和情感能力。
    AI-accelerated neuroscience could cure most mental illness and greatly enhance cognitive and emotional abilities.
  • 人工智能可通过优化的健康干预和物流推动贫困国家的经济发展。
    AI can drive economic development in poor countries through optimized health interventions and logistics.
  • 在生物学和神经科学中，智能的边际回报很高，关键发现常由少数天才研究者做出。
    The marginal returns to intelligence are high in biology and neuroscience, where key discoveries are often made by a few talented researchers.
  • 人工智能可作为虚拟生物学家，设计和运行实验，而不仅仅是分析数据。
    AI can act as a virtual biologist, designing and running experiments, not just analyzing data.
  • “压缩的21世纪”框架预测，在强大人工智能出现后的十年内，健康、寿命和生物自由将发生根本性改善。
    The 'compressed 21st century' framework predicts radical improvements in health, lifespan, and biological freedom within a decade of powerful AI.

局限 · Limitations

  • 预测是推测性的，细节可能出错；实验时间和物理定律等现实约束限制了速度。
    Predictions are speculative and may be wrong in details; real-world constraints like experiment times and physical laws limit speed.
  • 尽管人工智能取得进展，临床试验和监管官僚主义可能减缓新疗法的部署。
    Clinical trials and regulatory bureaucracy may slow deployment of new treatments despite AI advances.
  • 经济发展面临腐败和制度薄弱等人类约束，人工智能可能不易克服。
    Economic development faces human constraints like corruption and weak institutions, which AI may not easily overcome.
  • 尽管人工智能取得进展，快速进化的病原体或罕见癌症等疾病可能持续存在。
    Some diseases like fast-evolving pathogens or rare cancers may persist despite AI progress.
  • 意识上传等激进的神经科学应用可能超出5-10年的时间窗口。
    Mind uploading and other radical neuroscience applications likely lie outside the 5-10 year window.
