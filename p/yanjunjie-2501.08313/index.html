MiniMax-01：基于闪电注意力扩展基础模型

MiniMax-01: Scaling Foundation Models with Lightning Attention

摘要 · Abstract

我们推出了MiniMax-01系列，包括MiniMax-Text-01和MiniMax-VL-01，它们与顶级模型性能相当，同时在处理更长上下文方面具有卓越能力。核心在于闪电注意力及其高效扩展。为了最大化计算能力，我们将其与混合专家模型（MoE）结合，创建了一个拥有32个专家、总计4560亿参数的模型，其中每个token激活459亿参数。我们为MoE和闪电注意力开发了优化的并行策略和高效的计算-通信重叠技术。这种方法使我们能够在数百万token的上下文中，对数千亿参数的模型进行高效训练和推理。MiniMax-Text-01的上下文窗口在训练时可达100万token，并在推理时以可承受的成本外推至400万token。我们的视觉语言模型MiniMax-VL-01通过持续训练5120亿视觉语言token构建而成。在标准基准和内部基准上的实验表明，我们的模型与GPT-4o和Claude-3.5-Sonnet等最先进模型性能相当，同时提供20-32倍更长的上下文窗口。我们在https://github.com/MiniMax-AI上公开发布了MiniMax-01。

We introduce MiniMax-01 series, including MiniMax-Text-01 and MiniMax-VL-01, which are comparable to top-tier models while offering superior capabilities in processing longer contexts. The core lies in lightning attention and its efficient scaling. To maximize computational capacity, we integrate it with Mixture of Experts (MoE), creating a model with 32 experts and 456 billion total parameters, of which 45.9 billion are activated for each token. We develop an optimized parallel strategy and highly efficient computation-communication overlap techniques for MoE and lightning attention. This approach enables us to conduct efficient training and inference on models with hundreds of billions of parameters across contexts spanning millions of tokens. The context window of MiniMax-Text-01 can reach up to 1 million tokens during training and extrapolate to 4 million tokens during inference at an affordable cost. Our vision-language model, MiniMax-VL-01 is built through continued training with 512 billion vision-language tokens. Experiments on both standard and in-house benchmarks show that our models match the performance of state-of-the-art models like GPT-4o and Claude-3.5-Sonnet while offering 20-32 times longer context window. We publicly release MiniMax-01 at https://github.com/MiniMax-AI.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 引入闪电注意力机制，实现基础模型的高效规模扩张。
    Introduces lightning attention for efficient scaling of foundation models.
  • 将闪电注意力与混合专家模型（MoE）结合，总参数量达4560亿。
    Integrates lightning attention with Mixture of Experts (MoE) for 456B total parameters.
  • 为MoE和闪电注意力开发了优化的并行策略与高效计算通信重叠技术。
    Develops optimized parallel strategy and computation-communication overlap for MoE and lightning attention.
  • 以可承受成本实现100万词元的训练上下文窗口和400万词元的推理上下文窗口。
    Achieves 1M token training context window and 4M token inference context window at affordable cost.
  • 通过5120亿视觉语言词元的持续训练构建了MiniMax-VL-01视觉语言模型。
    Builds MiniMax-VL-01 vision-language model via continued training on 512B vision-language tokens.
  • 性能媲美GPT-4o和Claude-3.5-Sonnet，同时提供20-32倍更长的上下文窗口。
    Matches GPT-4o and Claude-3.5-Sonnet performance while offering 20-32x longer context window.

局限 · Limitations

  • 评估仅限于标准及内部基准测试，未涉及实际部署场景。
    Evaluation limited to standard and in-house benchmarks, not real-world deployment.
  • 上下文窗口外推至400万词元时，超长序列的质量可能下降。
    Context window extrapolation to 4M tokens may degrade quality for very long sequences.
  • 模型规模及MoE架构在训练和推理中需要大量算力资源。
    Model size and MoE architecture require significant computational resources for training and inference.
  • 视觉语言模型性能可能受限于训练数据的质量与多样性。
    Vision-language model performance may be constrained by the quality and diversity of training data.
  • 对训练分布外领域的泛化能力未经过充分测试。
    Generalization to domains outside training distribution is not thoroughly tested.
