阐明扩散生成模型的设计空间

Elucidating the Design Space of Diffusion-Based Generative Models

泰罗·卡拉斯 Tero Karras · NVIDIA · 2022-06-01 · arXiv:2206.00364 ↗ · 被引 3521

摘要 · Abstract

我们认为，当前扩散生成模型的理论和实践不必要地复杂，并试图通过提出一个清晰分离具体设计选择的设计空间来改善这一状况。这使我们能够识别采样和训练过程中的若干变化，以及分数网络的预处理。综合这些改进，我们在CIFAR-10上实现了新的最先进FID：类条件设置为1.79，无条件设置为1.97，且采样速度更快（每张图像仅需35次网络评估）。为了进一步展示其模块化特性，我们证明我们的设计变更显著提高了先前工作中预训练分数网络的效率和质量，包括将先前训练的ImageNet-64模型的FID从2.07提升至接近最先进的1.55，并在使用我们提出的改进重新训练后达到新的最先进水平1.36。

We argue that the theory and practice of diffusion-based generative models are currently unnecessarily convoluted and seek to remedy the situation by presenting a design space that clearly separates the concrete design choices. This lets us identify several changes to both the sampling and training processes, as well as preconditioning of the score networks. Together, our improvements yield new state-of-the-art FID of 1.79 for CIFAR-10 in a class-conditional setting and 1.97 in an unconditional setting, with much faster sampling (35 network evaluations per image) than prior designs. To further demonstrate their modular nature, we show that our design changes dramatically improve both the efficiency and quality obtainable with pre-trained score networks from previous work, including improving the FID of a previously trained ImageNet-64 model from 2.07 to near-SOTA 1.55, and after re-training with our proposed improvements to a new SOTA of 1.36.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出了扩散生成模型的解耦设计空间，将具体设计选择分离。
    Presents a disentangled design space for diffusion-based generative models, separating concrete design choices.
  • 识别出采样、训练和分数网络预处理的改进。
    Identifies improvements to sampling, training, and score network preconditioning.
  • 在CIFAR-10上实现了新的最优FID：有类别条件1.79，无类别条件1.97。
    Achieves new state-of-the-art FID of 1.79 on CIFAR-10 class-conditional and 1.97 unconditional.
  • 实现更快的采样，每张图像仅需35次网络评估。
    Enables much faster sampling with 35 network evaluations per image.
  • 改进预训练分数网络，例如ImageNet-64的FID从2.07提升至1.55。
    Improves pre-trained score networks, e.g., ImageNet-64 FID from 2.07 to 1.55.
  • 重新训练后，在ImageNet-64上达到新的最优FID 1.36。
    After retraining, achieves new SOTA FID of 1.36 on ImageNet-64.

局限 · Limitations

  • 评估仅限于图像生成任务（CIFAR-10、ImageNet-64）。
    Evaluation limited to image generation tasks (CIFAR-10, ImageNet-64).
  • 设计空间可能无法泛化到文本或音频等其他数据模态。
    Design space may not generalize to other data modalities like text or audio.
  • 改进依赖于特定的网络架构和超参数。
    Improvements rely on specific network architectures and hyperparameters.
  • 大规模模型的重新训练计算成本可能较高。
    Computational cost of retraining may be high for large-scale models.
  • 对设计选择的理论理解仍不完整。
    Theoretical understanding of design choices remains incomplete.
