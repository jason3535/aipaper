核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 使用GPT-4的顾问完成任务数量多12.2%，速度快25.1%，质量高40%。
    Consultants using GPT-4 completed 12.2% more tasks, 25.1% faster, with 40% higher quality.
  • AI作为技能均衡器，低绩效者提升43%，高于高绩效者。
    AI acts as a skill leveler, boosting low performers by 43% more than top performers.
  • “锯齿状前沿”概念：AI能力不均，任务在不可见边界内外。
    The 'Jagged Frontier' concept: AI capability is uneven, with tasks inside/outside an invisible boundary.
  • 两种有效的人机协作模式：半人马（明确分工）和赛博格（深度融合）。
    Two effective human-AI collaboration modes: Centaur (clear task division) and Cyborg (deep integration).
  • AI可能导致“方向盘上睡着”，在边界外任务上降低准确性。
    AI can cause 'falling asleep at the wheel', reducing accuracy on tasks outside its frontier.
  • AI输出质量更高但更同质；人机协作产生更好的多样性和正确性。
    AI outputs are higher quality but more homogeneous; human-AI collaboration yields better variety and correctness.

局限 · Limitations

  • 研究仅限于精英顾问；结果可能不适用于其他职业或情境。
    Study limited to elite consultants; results may not generalize to other professions or contexts.
  • AI的“锯齿状前沿”是动态且模型特定的；发现可能不适用于未来模型。
    AI's 'Jagged Frontier' is dynamic and model-specific; findings may not hold for future models.
  • 实验通过API使用GPT-4；不同界面或微调可能改变结果。
    Experiment used GPT-4 via API; different interfaces or fine-tuning could alter outcomes.
  • AI导致的输出同质化可能减少创新和问题解决的多样性。
    AI-induced homogeneity in outputs may reduce innovation and diversity in problem-solving.
  • 研究未探讨对技能发展或组织动态的长期影响。
    The study did not explore long-term effects on skill development or organizational dynamics.
