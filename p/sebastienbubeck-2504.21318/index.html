摘要 · Abstract

我们介绍了 Phi-4-reasoning，一个 140 亿参数的推理模型，在复杂推理任务上取得了强劲性能。该模型通过对 Phi-4 进行监督微调训练而成，训练数据包括精心挑选的“可教学”提示（具有适当的复杂度和多样性）以及使用 o3-mini 生成的推理演示。Phi-4-reasoning 能生成详细的推理链，有效利用推理时的计算资源。我们进一步开发了 Phi-4-reasoning-plus，通过短期的基于结果的强化学习增强，生成更长的推理轨迹，从而获得更高性能。在广泛的推理任务中，这两个模型都显著优于更大的开源模型，如 DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Llama-70B，并接近完整版 DeepSeek-R1 模型的性能水平。我们的综合评估涵盖了数学和科学推理、编码、算法问题求解、规划和空间理解等基准测试。有趣的是，我们还观察到这些改进非平凡地迁移到了通用基准测试中。在本报告中，我们提供了关于训练数据、训练方法和评估的见解。我们表明，精心策划的数据对监督微调（SFT）的益处也适用于推理语言模型，并且可以通过强化学习（RL）进一步放大。最后，我们的评估指出了改进推理模型性能和鲁棒性评估方法的机会。

We introduce Phi-4-reasoning, a 14-billion parameter reasoning model that achieves strong performance on complex reasoning tasks. Trained via supervised fine-tuning of Phi-4 on carefully curated set of "teachable" prompts-selected for the right level of complexity and diversity-and reasoning demonstrations generated using o3-mini, Phi-4-reasoning generates detailed reasoning chains that effectively leverage inference-time compute. We further develop Phi-4-reasoning-plus, a variant enhanced through a short phase of outcome-based reinforcement learning that offers higher performance by generating longer reasoning traces. Across a wide range of reasoning tasks, both models outperform significantly larger open-weight models such as DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Llama-70B model and approach the performance levels of full DeepSeek-R1 model. Our comprehensive evaluations span benchmarks in math and scientific reasoning, coding, algorithmic problem solving, planning, and spatial understanding. Interestingly, we observe a non-trivial transfer of improvements to general-purpose benchmarks as well. In this report, we provide insights into our training data, our training methodologies, and our evaluations. We show that the benefit of careful data curation for supervised fine-tuning (SFT) extends to reasoning language models, and can be further amplified by reinforcement learning (RL). Finally, our evaluation points to opportunities for improving how we assess the performance and robustness of reasoning models.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出Phi-4-reasoning，一个140亿参数模型，通过在精心策划的可教学提示上进行监督微调，在复杂推理任务中表现出色。
    Introduces Phi-4-reasoning, a 14B parameter model excelling in complex reasoning via SFT on curated teachable prompts.
  • 开发Phi-4-reasoning-plus，通过基于结果的强化学习增强，生成更长的推理轨迹并提升性能。
    Develops Phi-4-reasoning-plus, enhanced by outcome-based RL for longer reasoning traces and higher performance.
  • 在多种推理任务上超越显著更大的开源模型，如DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Llama-70B。
    Outperforms significantly larger open-weight models like DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Llama-70B on diverse reasoning tasks.
  • 在数学、科学、编程、规划和空间理解基准上接近完整DeepSeek-R1模型的性能水平。
    Approaches performance of full DeepSeek-R1 model across math, science, coding, planning, and spatial benchmarks.
  • 展示了推理改进向通用基准的非平凡迁移。
    Demonstrates non-trivial transfer of reasoning improvements to general-purpose benchmarks.
  • 提供了关于训练数据策划、监督微调和强化学习方法以及评估机会的见解。
    Provides insights into training data curation, SFT and RL methodologies, and evaluation opportunities.

局限 · Limitations

  • 模型规模限制在140亿参数，可能约束处理极端复杂任务的能力。
    Model size limited to 14B parameters, potentially constraining capacity for extremely complex tasks.
  • 依赖o3-mini生成推理演示可能引入偏差或质量差异。
    Reliance on o3-mini for generating reasoning demonstrations may introduce biases or quality variance.
  • 基于结果的强化学习关注最终正确性，可能忽略中间推理质量。
    Outcome-based RL focuses on final correctness, potentially neglecting intermediate reasoning quality.
  • 评估基准可能未完全捕捉鲁棒性或对分布外场景的泛化能力。
    Evaluation benchmarks may not fully capture robustness or generalization to out-of-distribution scenarios.
  • 观察到向通用基准的迁移但未完全解释或优化。
    Transfer to general-purpose benchmarks observed but not fully explained or optimized.
