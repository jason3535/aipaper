ImageBind: 一个嵌入空间绑定所有模态

ImageBind: One Embedding Space To Bind Them All

罗斯·吉尔希克 Ross Girshick · Meta AI · 2023-05-09 · arXiv:2305.05665 ↗

摘要 · Abstract

我们提出了ImageBind，一种学习跨六种不同模态（图像、文本、音频、深度、热成像和IMU数据）的联合嵌入的方法。我们表明，训练这样的联合嵌入并不需要所有配对数据的组合，仅图像配对数据就足以将模态绑定在一起。ImageBind可以利用最近的大规模视觉语言模型，并通过利用它们与图像的自然配对，将其零样本能力扩展到新模态。它实现了开箱即用的新兴应用，包括跨模态检索、模态算术组合、跨模态检测和生成。这些新兴能力随着图像编码器的强度而提高，我们在跨模态的零样本识别任务上设立了新的最先进水平，超越了专业监督模型。最后，我们展示了强大的少样本识别结果，优于先前的工作，并且ImageBind为评估视觉模型在视觉和非视觉任务上提供了一种新方法。

We present ImageBind, an approach to learn a joint embedding across six different modalities - images, text, audio, depth, thermal, and IMU data. We show that all combinations of paired data are not necessary to train such a joint embedding, and only image-paired data is sufficient to bind the modalities together. ImageBind can leverage recent large scale vision-language models, and extends their zero-shot capabilities to new modalities just by using their natural pairing with images. It enables novel emergent applications 'out-of-the-box' including cross-modal retrieval, composing modalities with arithmetic, cross-modal detection and generation. The emergent capabilities improve with the strength of the image encoder and we set a new state-of-the-art on emergent zero-shot recognition tasks across modalities, outperforming specialist supervised models. Finally, we show strong few-shot recognition results outperforming prior work, and that ImageBind serves as a new way to evaluate vision models for visual and non-visual tasks.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出ImageBind，仅使用图像配对数据实现六模态联合嵌入。
    Proposes ImageBind, a joint embedding across six modalities using only image-paired data.
  • 通过图像配对将视觉语言模型的零样本能力扩展到新模态。
    Extends zero-shot capabilities of vision-language models to new modalities via image pairing.
  • 实现跨模态检索、算术组合、检测和生成等新兴应用。
    Enables emergent applications like cross-modal retrieval, arithmetic composition, detection, and generation.
  • 在跨模态新兴零样本识别上达到新最优，超越监督模型。
    Sets new state-of-the-art on emergent zero-shot recognition across modalities, surpassing supervised models.
  • 展示强少样本识别结果，优于先前工作。
    Demonstrates strong few-shot recognition results outperforming prior work.
  • 为视觉模型在视觉和非视觉任务上提供新评估方法。
    Provides a new evaluation method for vision models on visual and non-visual tasks.

局限 · Limitations

  • 需要图像作为绑定模态；非图像配对模态可能未完全对齐。
    Requires image as a binding modality; non-image paired modalities may not be fully aligned.
  • 新兴能力依赖于图像编码器的强度。
    Emergent capabilities depend on the strength of the image encoder.
  • 限于六种模态；未探索扩展到其他模态。
    Limited to six modalities; extension to other modalities not explored.
  • 对于与图像相关性弱的模态，零样本性能可能下降。
    Zero-shot performance may degrade for modalities with weak image correlation.
  • 少样本结果依赖有限数据；未验证对更大数据集的扩展性。
    Few-shot results rely on limited data; scalability to larger datasets not verified.
