深度上下文词表示

Deep contextualized word representations

马修·彼得斯 Matthew Peters · Allen Institute for AI · 2018-02-15 · arXiv:1802.05365 ↗ · 被引 12188

摘要 · Abstract

我们引入了一种新型的深度上下文词表示，它同时建模了（1）单词使用的复杂特征（如句法和语义），以及（2）这些用法在不同语言上下文中的变化（即建模多义性）。我们的词向量是从一个在大规模文本语料上预训练的深度双向语言模型（biLM）的内部状态学习得到的函数。我们展示了这些表示可以轻松添加到现有模型中，并在六个具有挑战性的自然语言处理问题（包括问答、文本蕴含和情感分析）上显著提升了当前最优结果。我们还进行了分析，表明暴露预训练网络的深层内部结构至关重要，这使得下游模型能够混合不同类型的半监督信号。

We introduce a new type of deep contextualized word representation that models both (1) complex characteristics of word use (e.g., syntax and semantics), and (2) how these uses vary across linguistic contexts (i.e., to model polysemy). Our word vectors are learned functions of the internal states of a deep bidirectional language model (biLM), which is pre-trained on a large text corpus. We show that these representations can be easily added to existing models and significantly improve the state of the art across six challenging NLP problems, including question answering, textual entailment and sentiment analysis. We also present an analysis showing that exposing the deep internals of the pre-trained network is crucial, allowing downstream models to mix different types of semi-supervision signals.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出ELMo，一种基于双向语言模型的深度上下文词表示。
    Introduces ELMo, deep contextualized word representations from a bidirectional language model.
  • 展示ELMo在六个NLP任务（问答、文本蕴含、语义角色标注、共指消解、命名实体识别、情感分析）上提升最先进水平。
    Shows ELMo improves state-of-the-art on six NLP tasks: QA, textual entailment, SRL, coreference, NER, sentiment.
  • 证明使用所有biLM层优于仅使用顶层。
    Demonstrates that using all biLM layers outperforms using only the top layer.
  • 揭示biLM低层编码句法，高层编码语义。
    Reveals lower biLM layers encode syntax, higher layers encode semantics.
  • 展示ELMo提高样本效率，需要更少数据和更少更新。
    Shows ELMo increases sample efficiency, requiring less data and fewer updates.

局限 · Limitations

  • ELMo需要预训练的biLM，训练计算成本高。
    ELMo requires a pre-trained biLM, which is computationally expensive to train.
  • ELMo表示在预训练后固定，不对下游任务微调biLM。
    ELMo representations are fixed after pre-training; no fine-tuning of biLM for downstream tasks.
  • ELMo仅在英语上测试，其他语言性能未知。
    ELMo only tested on English; performance on other languages is unknown.
  • ELMo在推理时因biLM前向传播增加计算开销。
    ELMo adds computational overhead at inference time due to biLM forward pass.
