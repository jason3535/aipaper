RDT-1B：面向双臂操作任务的扩散基础模型

RDT-1B: a Diffusion Foundation Model for Bimanual Manipulation

刘松铭 Songming Liu · Tsinghua University · 2024-10-10 · arXiv:2410.07864 ↗ · 被引 725

摘要 · Abstract

双臂操作在机器人领域至关重要，但由于协调两个机械臂的固有复杂性（导致多模态动作分布）以及训练数据的稀缺，开发基础模型极具挑战性。本文提出了机器人扩散变换器（RDT），这是一种开创性的双臂操作扩散基础模型。RDT基于扩散模型有效表示多模态性，通过可扩展变换器的创新设计处理多模态输入的异质性，并捕捉机器人数据的非线性和高频特性。为解决数据稀缺问题，我们进一步引入了物理可解释的统一动作空间，该空间能够统一各种机器人的动作表示，同时保留原始动作的物理意义，促进可迁移物理知识的学习。凭借这些设计，我们成功在迄今为止最大的多机器人数据集上预训练了RDT，并将其扩展至12亿参数，这是目前最大的基于扩散的机器人操作基础模型。最后，我们在自创的包含6000多个片段的多任务双臂数据集上对RDT进行微调，以完善其操作能力。真实机器人实验表明，RDT显著优于现有方法。它能够零样本泛化到未见过的物体和场景，理解并遵循语言指令，仅需1~5次演示即可学习新技能，并有效处理复杂灵巧的任务。代码和视频请参见https://rdt-robotics.github.io/rdt-robotics/。

Bimanual manipulation is essential in robotics, yet developing foundation models is extremely challenging due to the inherent complexity of coordinating two robot arms (leading to multi-modal action distributions) and the scarcity of training data. In this paper, we present the Robotics Diffusion Transformer (RDT), a pioneering diffusion foundation model for bimanual manipulation. RDT builds on diffusion models to effectively represent multi-modality, with innovative designs of a scalable Transformer to deal with the heterogeneity of multi-modal inputs and to capture the nonlinearity and high frequency of robotic data. To address data scarcity, we further introduce a Physically Interpretable Unified Action Space, which can unify the action representations of various robots while preserving the physical meanings of original actions, facilitating learning transferrable physical knowledge. With these designs, we managed to pre-train RDT on the largest collection of multi-robot datasets to date and scaled it up to 1.2B parameters, which is the largest diffusion-based foundation model for robotic manipulation. We finally fine-tuned RDT on a self-created multi-task bimanual dataset with over 6K+ episodes to refine its manipulation capabilities. Experiments on real robots demonstrate that RDT significantly outperforms existing methods. It exhibits zero-shot generalization to unseen objects and scenes, understands and follows language instructions, learns new skills with just 1~5 demonstrations, and effectively handles complex, dexterous tasks. We refer to https://rdt-robotics.github.io/rdt-robotics/ for the code and videos.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出机器人扩散Transformer（RDT），一种用于双臂操作的扩散基础模型，具有可扩展的Transformer架构。
    Proposes Robotics Diffusion Transformer (RDT), a diffusion foundation model for bimanual manipulation with scalable Transformer architecture.
  • 引入物理可解释统一动作空间，统一不同机器人的动作表示，实现多机器人预训练。
    Introduces Physically Interpretable Unified Action Space to unify action representations across diverse robots for multi-robot pre-training.
  • 在双臂任务上实现最先进性能，成功率比基线提高56%。
    Achieves state-of-the-art performance on bimanual tasks with 56% improvement in success rates over baselines.
  • 展示了对未见物体、场景和语言指令的零样本泛化能力。
    Demonstrates zero-shot generalization to unseen objects, scenes, and language instructions.
  • 仅需1-5次演示即可实现新技能的少样本学习。
    Enables few-shot learning of new skills with only 1-5 demonstrations.
  • 将模型扩展到12亿参数，是最大的基于扩散的机器人操作基础模型。
    Scales model to 1.2B parameters, the largest diffusion-based foundation model for robotic manipulation.

局限 · Limitations

  • 模型仅在ALOHA双臂机器人上评估，未测试对其他双臂机器人的泛化性。
    Model is only evaluated on ALOHA dual-arm robot; generalization to other dual-arm robots is not tested.
  • 微调数据集为自收集，仅6000多条轨迹，可能无法覆盖所有真实场景。
    Fine-tuning dataset is self-collected and limited to 6K+ trajectories, which may not cover all real-world scenarios.
  • 预训练依赖公开的多机器人数据集，可能包含偏差或噪声。
    Pre-training relies on publicly available multi-robot datasets, which may contain biases or noise.
  • 扩散模型推理需要多个去噪步骤，导致延迟高于确定性方法。
    Diffusion model inference requires multiple denoising steps, leading to higher latency compared to deterministic methods.
  • 统一动作空间仅针对夹爪臂设计，不支持其他末端执行器的机器人。
    The unified action space is designed for gripper arms only; robots with other end-effectors are not supported.
