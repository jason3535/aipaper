分析与改进扩散模型的训练动态

Analyzing and Improving the Training Dynamics of Diffusion Models

泰罗·卡拉斯 Tero Karras · NVIDIA · 2023-12-05 · arXiv:2312.02696 ↗ · 被引 431

摘要 · Abstract

扩散模型目前凭借其在大数据集上无与伦比的扩展能力，主导了数据驱动的图像合成领域。在本文中，我们识别并纠正了流行的ADM扩散模型架构中导致训练不均匀和低效的几个原因，同时不改变其高层结构。观察到训练过程中网络激活和权重出现不受控制的幅度变化和不平衡，我们重新设计了网络层，以在期望上保持激活、权重和更新的幅度。我们发现，系统应用这一理念消除了观察到的漂移和不平衡，从而在相同计算复杂度下获得了显著更好的网络。我们的改进将ImageNet-512合成中先前2.41的FID记录提升至1.81，且使用了快速确定性采样。作为一项独立贡献，我们提出了一种事后（即完成训练后）设置指数移动平均（EMA）参数的方法。这使得无需进行多次训练即可精确调整EMA长度，并揭示了其与网络架构、训练时间和引导的惊人交互作用。

Diffusion models currently dominate the field of data-driven image synthesis with their unparalleled scaling to large datasets. In this paper, we identify and rectify several causes for uneven and ineffective training in the popular ADM diffusion model architecture, without altering its high-level structure. Observing uncontrolled magnitude changes and imbalances in both the network activations and weights over the course of training, we redesign the network layers to preserve activation, weight, and update magnitudes on expectation. We find that systematic application of this philosophy eliminates the observed drifts and imbalances, resulting in considerably better networks at equal computational complexity. Our modifications improve the previous record FID of 2.41 in ImageNet-512 synthesis to 1.81, achieved using fast deterministic sampling. As an independent contribution, we present a method for setting the exponential moving average (EMA) parameters post-hoc, i.e., after completing the training run. This allows precise tuning of EMA length without the cost of performing several training runs, and reveals its surprising interactions with network architecture, training time, and guidance.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 识别并纠正了ADM扩散模型中训练不均匀的原因。
    Identifies and rectifies causes of uneven training in ADM diffusion models.
  • 重新设计网络层以保持激活、权重和更新幅度的期望值。
    Redesigns network layers to preserve activation, weight, and update magnitudes.
  • 在ImageNet-512上使用快速确定性采样实现了1.81的创纪录FID。
    Achieves record FID of 1.81 on ImageNet-512 with fast deterministic sampling.
  • 提出无需额外训练的后验EMA参数调整方法。
    Proposes post-hoc EMA parameter tuning without additional training runs.
  • 揭示了EMA与架构、训练时间和引导的相互作用。
    Reveals interactions of EMA with architecture, training time, and guidance.

局限 · Limitations

  • 修改针对ADM架构，未在其他扩散模型上测试。
    Modifications are specific to ADM architecture; not tested on other diffusion models.
  • 后验EMA调整假设训练运行固定，可能不适用于变化的时间表。
    Post-hoc EMA tuning assumes fixed training run; may not generalize to varying schedules.
  • 改进仅在ImageNet上展示，对其他数据集的泛化性未知。
    Improvements demonstrated only on ImageNet; generalizability to other datasets is unknown.
  • 方法未改变高层结构，ADM的基本局限性依然存在。
    Method does not alter high-level structure; fundamental limitations of ADM remain.
