事后经验回放

Hindsight Experience Replay

摘要 · Abstract

处理稀疏奖励是强化学习中最具挑战性的问题之一。我们提出了一种名为“事后经验回放”的新技术，该技术能够从稀疏且二元的奖励中进行样本高效的学习，从而避免了对复杂奖励工程的需求。它可以与任意离策略强化学习算法结合使用，并可视为一种隐式课程学习。我们在机械臂操作物体的任务上展示了该方法的效果。具体来说，我们在三个不同的任务上进行了实验：推、滑动和抓取放置，每个任务仅使用指示任务是否完成的二元奖励。我们的消融研究表明，事后经验回放是在这些具有挑战性的环境中实现训练的关键因素。我们展示了在物理仿真中训练的策略可以部署到实体机器人上并成功完成任务。

Dealing with sparse rewards is one of the biggest challenges in Reinforcement Learning (RL). We present a novel technique called Hindsight Experience Replay which allows sample-efficient learning from rewards which are sparse and binary and therefore avoid the need for complicated reward engineering. It can be combined with an arbitrary off-policy RL algorithm and may be seen as a form of implicit curriculum. We demonstrate our approach on the task of manipulating objects with a robotic arm. In particular, we run experiments on three different tasks: pushing, sliding, and pick-and-place, in each case using only binary rewards indicating whether or not the task is completed. Our ablation studies show that Hindsight Experience Replay is a crucial ingredient which makes training possible in these challenging environments. We show that our policies trained on a physics simulation can be deployed on a physical robot and successfully complete the task.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出后见经验回放（HER），实现从稀疏二元奖励中进行样本高效学习。
    Proposes Hindsight Experience Replay (HER) for sample-efficient learning from sparse binary rewards.
  • 通过使用二元任务完成信号，消除了复杂奖励工程的需求。
    Eliminates the need for complex reward engineering by using binary task-completion signals.
  • 可与任意离策略强化学习算法结合，充当隐式课程。
    Can be combined with any off-policy RL algorithm, acting as an implicit curriculum.
  • 在推动、滑动和抓取放置三个机器人操作任务上展示了成功。
    Demonstrates success on three robotic manipulation tasks: pushing, sliding, and pick-and-place.
  • 消融研究证实HER在挑战性稀疏奖励环境中对训练至关重要。
    Ablation studies confirm HER is crucial for training in challenging sparse-reward environments.
  • 在仿真中训练的策略可迁移到物理机器人上完成任务。
    Policies trained in simulation transfer to a physical robot for task completion.

局限 · Limitations

  • HER假设目标状态在回合内可达，限制了适用性。
    HER assumes that goal states are reachable within the episode, limiting applicability.
  • 需要目标条件化的值函数或策略，增加了架构约束。
    Requires a goal-conditioned value function or policy, adding architectural constraints.
  • 当目标远离初始状态时，由于探索困难，性能可能下降。
    Performance may degrade when goals are far from initial states due to exploration difficulty.
  • 仅在机器人操作任务上评估，未测试对其他领域的泛化性。
    Evaluated only on robotic manipulation tasks; generalization to other domains is untested.
  • 依赖二元奖励，可能无法捕捉细微的任务需求。
    Relies on binary rewards, which may not capture nuanced task requirements.
