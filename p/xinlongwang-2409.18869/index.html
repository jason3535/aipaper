Emu3：只需预测下一个Token

Emu3: Next-Token Prediction is All You Need

摘要 · Abstract

虽然下一个Token预测被认为是通往通用人工智能的有前途的路径，但在多模态任务中，它一直难以取得优异表现，这些任务仍然由扩散模型（如Stable Diffusion）和组合方法（如CLIP结合LLM）主导。在本文中，我们介绍了Emu3，一套仅通过下一个Token预测训练的最先进多模态模型。通过将图像、文本和视频分词到离散空间，我们在多模态序列的混合上从头训练了一个单一的Transformer。Emu3在生成和感知任务上超越了多个成熟的任务特定模型，超过了SDXL和LLaVA-1.6等旗舰模型，同时消除了对扩散或组合架构的需求。Emu3还能够通过预测视频序列中的下一个Token来生成高保真视频。我们通过专注于一个单一焦点——Token，简化了复杂的多模态模型设计，在训练和推理过程中释放了巨大的扩展潜力。我们的结果表明，下一个Token预测是构建超越语言的通用多模态智能的一条有前途的路径。我们开源了关键技术和模型，以支持这一方向的进一步研究。

While next-token prediction is considered a promising path towards artificial general intelligence, it has struggled to excel in multimodal tasks, which are still dominated by diffusion models (e.g., Stable Diffusion) and compositional approaches (e.g., CLIP combined with LLMs). In this paper, we introduce Emu3, a new suite of state-of-the-art multimodal models trained solely with next-token prediction. By tokenizing images, text, and videos into a discrete space, we train a single transformer from scratch on a mixture of multimodal sequences. Emu3 outperforms several well-established task-specific models in both generation and perception tasks, surpassing flagship models such as SDXL and LLaVA-1.6, while eliminating the need for diffusion or compositional architectures. Emu3 is also capable of generating high-fidelity video via predicting the next token in a video sequence. We simplify complex multimodal model designs by converging on a singular focus: tokens, unlocking great potential for scaling both during training and inference. Our results demonstrate that next-token prediction is a promising path towards building general multimodal intelligence beyond language. We open-source key techniques and models to support further research in this direction.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出Emu3，一种仅通过下一个词预测训练的多模态模型，消除了扩散或组合架构。
    Introduces Emu3, a multimodal model trained solely with next-token prediction, eliminating diffusion or compositional architectures.
  • 将图像、文本和视频分词到离散空间，用于统一的Transformer训练。
    Tokenizes images, text, and videos into a discrete space for unified transformer training.
  • 在生成和感知任务上优于SDXL和LLaVA-1.6等专用模型。
    Outperforms task-specific models like SDXL and LLaVA-1.6 in both generation and perception tasks.
  • 通过视频序列中的下一个词预测实现高保真视频生成。
    Demonstrates high-fidelity video generation via next-token prediction in video sequences.
  • 通过仅关注token简化多模态模型设计，支持训练和推理时的规模扩张。
    Simplifies multimodal model design by focusing solely on tokens, enabling scaling during training and inference.
  • 开源关键技术和模型以支持进一步研究。
    Open-sources key techniques and models to support further research.

局限 · Limitations

  • 评估限于特定基准；对更广泛任务的泛化性未验证。
    Evaluation limited to specific benchmarks; generalizability to broader tasks unverified.
  • 与连续表示相比，连续模态的分词可能导致信息丢失。
    Tokenization of continuous modalities may cause information loss compared to continuous representations.
  • 大规模多模态模型的训练和推理成本仍然很高。
    Training and inference costs for large-scale multimodal models remain high.
  • 在复杂推理或长上下文任务上的性能未深入探索。
    Performance on complex reasoning or long-context tasks not thoroughly explored.
  • 依赖下一个词预测可能限制模型捕捉全局结构的能力。
    Reliance on next-token prediction may limit model's ability to capture global structure.
