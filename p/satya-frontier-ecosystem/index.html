A frontier without an ecosystem is not stable

摘要 · Abstract

关于技术进步和现实世界影响的笔记。

Notes on advances in technology and real-world impact

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出“Token 资本”概念，即企业拥有的 AI 能力，与人力资本复合增长。
    Introduces 'token capital' as firm-owned AI capability that compounds with human capital.
  • 主张人类能动性驱动 Token 资本增长；任务可外包，学习不可外包。
    Argues human agency drives token capital growth; offloading tasks never offloads learning.
  • 提出学习循环架构，通过智能体系统使人力与 Token 资本复合增长。
    Proposes a learning loop architecture where human and token capital compound via agentic systems.
  • 强调使用私有评估和强化学习环境，让模型针对业务特定结果改进。
    Emphasizes private evals and RL environments for models to improve on business-specific outcomes.
  • 警告价值集中于少数前沿模型的风险，倡导构建前沿生态系统。
    Warns against value concentration in few frontier models, advocating a frontier ecosystem.
  • 声称早期构建此学习循环的企业将获得难以复制的优势。
    Claims firms that build this learning loop early gain hard-to-replicate advantage.

局限 · Limitations

  • 缺乏实证证据；Token 资本和学习循环等概念未经验证。
    Lacks empirical evidence; concepts like token capital and learning loop are not validated.
  • 假设企业能构建专有 AI 系统而不依赖外部模型，可能不现实。
    Assumes firms can build proprietary AI systems without relying on external models.
  • 未解决将人类判断融入 AI 循环的技术挑战。
    Does not address technical challenges of integrating human judgment into AI loops.
  • 忽视了 AI 可能取代而非增强人类角色的可能性。
    Overlooks potential for AI to replace human roles, not just augment them.
  • 该愿景可能仅限于有资源构建此类系统的大型企业。
    The vision may be limited to large firms with resources to build such systems.
