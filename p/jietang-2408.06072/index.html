CogVideoX：基于专家Transformer的文生视频扩散模型

CogVideoX: Text-to-Video Diffusion Models with An Expert Transformer

摘要 · Abstract

我们提出了CogVideoX，一个基于扩散Transformer的大规模文生视频生成模型，能够生成与文本提示对齐的10秒连续视频，帧率为16fps，分辨率为768*1360像素。先前的视频生成模型通常运动有限、时长较短，且难以根据文本生成连贯叙事的视频。我们提出了几种设计来解决这些问题。首先，我们提出了一个3D变分自编码器（VAE），在空间和时间维度上压缩视频，以提高压缩率和视频保真度。其次，为了改善文本-视频对齐，我们提出了一个专家Transformer，采用专家自适应层归一化来促进两种模态的深度融合。第三，通过采用渐进训练和多分辨率帧打包技术，CogVideoX能够生成具有显著运动的连贯、长时长、不同形状的视频。此外，我们开发了一个有效的文本-视频数据处理流程，包括多种数据预处理策略和视频字幕方法，极大地提高了生成质量和语义对齐。结果表明，CogVideoX在多个机器指标和人类评估中均展现出最先进的性能。3D因果VAE、视频字幕模型和CogVideoX的模型权重已在https://github.com/THUDM/CogVideo上公开。

We present CogVideoX, a large-scale text-to-video generation model based on diffusion transformer, which can generate 10-second continuous videos aligned with text prompt, with a frame rate of 16 fps and resolution of 768 * 1360 pixels. Previous video generation models often had limited movement and short durations, and is difficult to generate videos with coherent narratives based on text. We propose several designs to address these issues. First, we propose a 3D Variational Autoencoder (VAE) to compress videos along both spatial and temporal dimensions, to improve both compression rate and video fidelity. Second, to improve the text-video alignment, we propose an expert transformer with the expert adaptive LayerNorm to facilitate the deep fusion between the two modalities. Third, by employing a progressive training and multi-resolution frame pack technique, CogVideoX is adept at producing coherent, long-duration, different shape videos characterized by significant motions. In addition, we develop an effective text-video data processing pipeline that includes various data preprocessing strategies and a video captioning method, greatly contributing to the generation quality and semantic alignment. Results show that CogVideoX demonstrates state-of-the-art performance across both multiple machine metrics and human evaluations. The model weight of both 3D Causal VAE, Video caption model and CogVideoX are publicly available at https://github.com/THUDM/CogVideo.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出3D变分自编码器（VAE），在空间和时间维度上压缩视频，提升压缩率和视频保真度。
    Proposes a 3D Variational Autoencoder (VAE) to compress videos along spatial and temporal dimensions, improving compression rate and video fidelity.
  • 引入带有专家自适应层归一化的专家Transformer，实现文本与视频模态的深度融合，增强文本-视频对齐。
    Introduces an expert Transformer with expert adaptive LayerNorm for deep fusion of text and video modalities, enhancing text-video alignment.
  • 采用渐进式训练和多分辨率帧打包技术，生成连贯、时长较长且运动显著的视频。
    Employs progressive training and multi-resolution frame pack technique to generate coherent, long-duration videos with significant motion.
  • 开发了包含数据预处理和视频字幕生成的有效文本-视频数据处理流程，提升生成质量和语义对齐。
    Develops an effective text-video data processing pipeline including data preprocessing and video captioning, improving generation quality and semantic alignment.
  • 在多个机器指标和人工评估上达到文本到视频生成的领先性能。
    Achieves state-of-the-art performance on multiple machine metrics and human evaluations for text-to-video generation.
  • 公开了3D因果VAE、视频字幕模型和CogVideoX的模型权重。
    Releases model weights of 3D Causal VAE, video caption model, and CogVideoX publicly.

局限 · Limitations

  • 仅支持生成10秒、16帧率、768x1360分辨率的视频，不支持更长或更高分辨率输出。
    Limited to generating 10-second videos at 16 fps and 768x1360 resolution, not supporting longer or higher-resolution outputs.
  • 由于大规模模型和3D VAE，训练和推理需要大量算力资源。
    Requires significant computational resources for training and inference due to large-scale model and 3D VAE.
  • 对于训练数据中未充分表示的复杂或抽象提示，文本-视频对齐可能下降。
    Text-video alignment may degrade for complex or abstract prompts not well-represented in training data.
  • 渐进式训练和多分辨率技术可能在某些生成视频中引入伪影或不一致。
    Progressive training and multi-resolution techniques may introduce artifacts or inconsistencies in some generated videos.
  • 数据处理流程依赖特定字幕生成方法，可能限制对多样化视频领域的泛化。
    Data processing pipeline relies on specific captioning methods, which may limit generalization to diverse video domains.
