智能时代

The Intelligence Age

萨姆·奥尔特曼 Sam Altman · OpenAI · 2024-09-23 · Sam Altman Essay ↗

摘要 · Abstract

在接下来的几十年里，我们将能够做到在祖父母看来如同魔法般的事情。这种现象并不新鲜，但将会以新的速度加速。随着时间的推移，人类的能力显著提升；我们现在已经能够完成前人认为不可能的事情。我们之所以更强大，不是因为基因变化，而是因为我们受益于社会基础设施，它比我们任何一个人都更聪明、更有能力；从重要意义上说，社会本身就是一种高级智能。我们的祖父母以及之前的几代人，建造并取得了伟大的成就。他们为人类进步的脚手架做出了贡献，我们都从中受益。人工智能将为人们提供解决难题的工具，并帮助我们在这个脚手架上添加我们自己无法想出的新支柱。进步的故事将继续，我们的孩子将能够做到我们做不到的事情。

In the next couple of decades, we will be able to do things that would have seemed like magic to our grandparents. This phenomenon is not new, but it will be newly accelerated. People have become dramatically more capable over time; we can already accomplish things now that our predecessors would have believed to be impossible. We are more capable not because of genetic change, but because we benefit from the infrastructure of society being way smarter and more capable than any one of us; in an important sense, society itself is a form of advanced intelligence. Our grandparents – and the generations that came before them – built and achieved great things. They contributed to the scaffolding of human progress that we all benefit from. AI will give people tools to solve hard problems and help us add new struts to that scaffolding that we couldn’t have figured out on our own. The story of progress will continue, and our children will be able to do things we can’t.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 深度学习有效，且随算力和数据可预测地扩展，能在几千天内实现超级智能。
    Deep learning works and scales predictably with compute and data, enabling superintelligence within a few thousand days.
  • AI将作为自主个人助理，改善医疗、教育和软件创作。
    AI will serve as autonomous personal assistants, improving healthcare, education, and software creation.
  • 智能时代将带来巨大的共享繁荣，使每个人的生活比今天任何人都好。
    The Intelligence Age will bring massive shared prosperity, making everyone's lives better than anyone's today.
  • 进步需要充足的算力和能源基础设施，避免AI成为富人的有限资源。
    Progress requires abundant compute and energy infrastructure to avoid AI becoming a limited resource for the rich.
  • AI将助力解决气候变化、太空殖民和完整物理学理解等重大挑战。
    AI will enable solving grand challenges like climate change, space colonization, and complete physics understanding.
  • 大多数工作变化缓慢，但AI放大人类创造力，促进正和社会博弈。
    Most jobs will change slowly, but AI amplifies human creativity and positive-sum societal games.

局限 · Limitations

  • 超级智能的“几千天”时间线是推测性的，可能过于乐观。
    The timeline of 'a few thousand days' for superintelligence is speculative and may be overly optimistic.
  • 文章缺乏将超级智能与人类价值观对齐的具体机制，这是一个重大开放问题。
    The essay lacks concrete mechanisms for aligning superintelligence with human values, a major open problem.
  • 对劳动力市场的潜在负面影响被承认但未深入分析，尤其是岗位替代。
    Potential negative impacts on labor markets are acknowledged but not deeply analyzed, especially job displacement.
  • 深度学习足以实现AGI的假设忽略了推理和因果理解等已知局限。
    The assumption that deep learning alone suffices for AGI ignores known limitations like reasoning and causal understanding.
  • 文章未涉及滥用风险，如自主武器或监控，这些风险可能超过收益。
    The essay does not address risks of misuse, such as autonomous weapons or surveillance, which could outweigh benefits.
