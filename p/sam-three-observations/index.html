核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • AGI 的智能与资源（训练算力、数据、推理算力）的对数成正比。
    AGI intelligence scales as log of resources (training compute, data, inference compute).
  • 使用特定水平 AI 的成本每 12 个月下降约 10 倍，快于摩尔定律。
    Cost to use a given AI level drops ~10x every 12 months, faster than Moore's Law.
  • 线性增长的智能带来的社会经济价值是超指数级的。
    Socioeconomic value of linearly increasing intelligence is super-exponential.
  • AI 智能体将成为虚拟同事，可扩展到数千或数百万个。
    AI agents will become virtual co-workers, scaling to thousands or millions.
  • AGI 将成为人类意志的最大杠杆，使个人影响力更大。
    AGI will be the biggest lever for human willfulness, enabling greater individual impact.
  • 开源和个体赋权是平衡安全与自由的关键。
    Open-sourcing and individual empowerment are key to balancing safety and freedom.

局限 · Limitations

  • 缩放定律可能在极端资源水平或新任务上失效。
    Scaling laws may break down at extreme resource levels or for novel tasks.
  • 成本下降趋势可能因物理或算法限制而放缓。
    Cost reduction trends may slow due to physical or algorithmic limits.
  • 超指数价值增长假设没有颠覆性的社会或监管变化。
    Super-exponential value growth assumes no disruptive societal or regulatory changes.
  • AI 智能体可能在需要创造力或长期规划的任务上失败。
    AI agents may fail on tasks requiring creativity or long-term planning.
  • 若无政策干预，AGI 收益分配不均可能加剧不平等。
    Uneven distribution of AGI benefits could worsen inequality without policy intervention.
