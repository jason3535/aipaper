作为设计师，我如何应对采用AI的压力

How I’m dealing with the pressure to adopt AI as a designer

马丁·赖特 Martin Wright · · 2026-02-15 · Martin Wright Blog ↗

摘要 · Abstract

本文探讨了设计师在面对AI技术普及时的压力与应对策略。作者认为，AI虽然能提高效率，但无法替代设计师的创造力、同理心和批判性思维。建议设计师保持好奇心，学习AI工具，同时强化自身独特价值，如用户研究、故事讲述和情感设计。最终，AI应被视为协作伙伴，而非威胁。

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 提出“六个月规则”，通过等待来过滤AI炒作，再评估工具。
    Proposes a 'six-month rule' to filter AI hype by waiting before evaluating tools.
  • 主张设计师应像评估任何工具一样评估AI：仅在提升工作质量时采用。
    Argues designers should evaluate AI like any tool: only adopt if it improves work quality.
  • 区分低风险的AI实验与高风险的AI生产部署。
    Distinguishes low-risk experimentation from high-stakes production deployment of AI.
  • 识别出“均值回归”风险：AI产生平庸输出，限制创新。
    Identifies 'regression to the mean' risk: AI produces average outputs, limiting innovation.
  • 引用Anthropic研究，表明AI辅助降低理解力和技能保持。
    Cites Anthropic study showing AI assistance reduces comprehension and skill retention.
  • 倡导保护设计的“中间层”（解释、综合、判断）免受AI侵蚀。
    Advocates protecting the 'middle layer' of design (interpretation, synthesis, judgement) from AI.

局限 · Limitations

  • 六个月规则可能错过快速演变且迅速变得必需的工具。
    The six-month rule may miss fast-evolving tools that become essential quickly.
  • 建议基于个人经验，而非AI采纳的实证研究。
    The advice is based on personal experience, not empirical research on AI adoption.
  • “均值回归”说法缺乏来自设计领域研究的直接证据。
    The 'regression to the mean' claim lacks direct evidence from design-specific studies.
  • 该方法可能无法扩展到风险承受能力不同的团队或组织。
    The approach may not scale to teams or organizations with different risk tolerances.
  • 文章未讨论AI对初级设计师学习手艺的潜在益处。
    The article does not address potential benefits of AI for junior designers learning craft.
