基于子词单元的罕见词神经机器翻译

Neural Machine Translation of Rare Words with Subword Units

里科·森里希 Rico Sennrich · University of Edinburgh · 2015-08-31 · arXiv:1508.07909 ↗ · 被引 8814

摘要 · Abstract

神经机器翻译（NMT）模型通常使用固定词汇表进行操作，但翻译是一个开放词汇问题。先前的工作通过回退到词典来处理词汇表外单词的翻译。在本文中，我们介绍了一种更简单且更有效的方法，通过将罕见词和未知词编码为子词单元序列，使NMT模型能够进行开放词汇翻译。这基于以下直觉：各种词类可以通过比单词更小的单元进行翻译，例如名称（通过字符复制或音译）、复合词（通过组合翻译）以及同源词和借词（通过语音和形态变换）。我们讨论了不同分词技术的适用性，包括简单的字符n-gram模型和基于字节对编码压缩算法的分词，并通过实验证明，在WMT 15英德和英俄翻译任务中，子词模型相比回退词典基线分别提高了1.1和1.3 BLEU。

Neural machine translation (NMT) models typically operate with a fixed vocabulary, but translation is an open-vocabulary problem. Previous work addresses the translation of out-of-vocabulary words by backing off to a dictionary. In this paper, we introduce a simpler and more effective approach, making the NMT model capable of open-vocabulary translation by encoding rare and unknown words as sequences of subword units. This is based on the intuition that various word classes are translatable via smaller units than words, for instance names (via character copying or transliteration), compounds (via compositional translation), and cognates and loanwords (via phonological and morphological transformations). We discuss the suitability of different word segmentation techniques, including simple character n-gram models and a segmentation based on the byte pair encoding compression algorithm, and empirically show that subword models improve over a back-off dictionary baseline for the WMT 15 translation tasks English-German and English-Russian by 1.1 and 1.3 BLEU, respectively.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 引入子词单元实现开放词汇的神经机器翻译，能够翻译稀有和未知词。
    Introduces subword units for open-vocabulary NMT, enabling translation of rare and unknown words.
  • 提出将稀有词编码为子词单元序列，利用字符复制、音译和组合翻译。
    Proposes encoding rare words as sequences of subword units, leveraging character copying, transliteration, and compositional translation.
  • 讨论了字符n-gram模型和字节对编码在子词切分中的适用性。
    Discusses suitability of character n-gram models and byte pair encoding for subword segmentation.
  • 在WMT 15英德和英俄任务上，相比回退词典基线分别提升1.1和1.3 BLEU。
    Achieves +1.1 BLEU on English-German and +1.3 BLEU on English-Russian WMT 15 tasks over back-off dictionary baseline.
  • 证明子词模型通过形态变换处理复合词、同源词和借词。
    Demonstrates that subword models handle compounds, cognates, and loanwords via morphological transformations.

局限 · Limitations

  • 评估仅限于WMT 15英德和英俄翻译任务。
    Evaluation limited to WMT 15 English-German and English-Russian translation tasks.
  • 子词切分可能对形态复杂的语言引入噪声。
    Subword segmentation may introduce noise for languages with complex morphology.
  • 字节对编码切分是启发式的，可能并非所有语言对的最优选择。
    Byte pair encoding segmentation is heuristic and may not be optimal for all language pairs.
  • 该方法未解决稀有词翻译中的语义歧义问题。
    The approach does not address semantic ambiguity in rare word translation.
