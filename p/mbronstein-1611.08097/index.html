几何深度学习：超越欧几里得数据

Geometric deep learning: going beyond Euclidean data

迈克尔·布朗斯坦 Michael Bronstein · USI Lugano · 2016-11-24 · arXiv:1611.08097 ↗ · 被引 3852

摘要 · Abstract

许多科学领域研究的数据具有非欧几里得空间的底层结构，例如计算社会科学中的社交网络、通信中的传感器网络、脑成像中的功能网络、遗传学中的调控网络以及计算机图形学中的网格曲面。这些几何数据通常规模庞大且复杂（如社交网络可达数十亿规模），自然成为机器学习技术的目标。特别是，我们希望能使用深度神经网络，这些网络已在计算机视觉、自然语言处理和音频分析等广泛问题上展现出强大能力。然而，这些工具在具有欧几里得或网格状结构的数据上最为成功，且其不变性被内置于用于建模的网络中。几何深度学习是一个总称，涵盖试图将（结构化的）深度神经网络模型推广到非欧几里得领域（如图和流形）的新兴技术。本文旨在概述几何深度学习问题的不同示例，并介绍现有解决方案、关键难点、应用以及这一新兴领域的未来研究方向。

Many scientific fields study data with an underlying structure that is a non-Euclidean space. Some examples include social networks in computational social sciences, sensor networks in communications, functional networks in brain imaging, regulatory networks in genetics, and meshed surfaces in computer graphics. In many applications, such geometric data are large and complex (in the case of social networks, on the scale of billions), and are natural targets for machine learning techniques. In particular, we would like to use deep neural networks, which have recently proven to be powerful tools for a broad range of problems from computer vision, natural language processing, and audio analysis. However, these tools have been most successful on data with an underlying Euclidean or grid-like structure, and in cases where the invariances of these structures are built into networks used to model them. Geometric deep learning is an umbrella term for emerging techniques attempting to generalize (structured) deep neural models to non-Euclidean domains such as graphs and manifolds. The purpose of this paper is to overview different examples of geometric deep learning problems and present available solutions, key difficulties, applications, and future research directions in this nascent field.

核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • 将几何深度学习作为处理非欧几里得数据（如图和流形）的框架引入。
    Introduces geometric deep learning as a framework for non-Euclidean data like graphs and manifolds.
  • 综述了社交网络、传感器网络、脑成像、遗传学和图形学中的关键应用。
    Reviews key applications in social networks, sensor networks, brain imaging, genetics, and graphics.
  • 指出了将深度神经网络推广到非欧几里得领域的困难。
    Identifies difficulties in generalizing deep neural networks to non-Euclidean domains.
  • 总结了几何深度学习问题的现有解决方案。
    Summarizes available solutions for geometric deep learning problems.
  • 勾勒了这一新兴领域的未来研究方向。
    Outlines future research directions in this nascent field.

局限 · Limitations

  • 专注于非欧几里得数据，排除了图像和文本等欧几里得结构数据。
    Focuses on non-Euclidean data, excluding Euclidean-structured data like images and text.
  • 提供高层次概述，缺乏深入的技术细节。
    Provides a high-level overview without in-depth technical details.
  • 未涵盖具体算法或实证比较。
    Does not cover specific algorithms or empirical comparisons.
  • 仅限于早期研究，许多挑战仍未解决。
    Limited to early-stage research; many challenges remain unsolved.
