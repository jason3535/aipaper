核心贡献 · Key contributions

  • AI 助手将成为下一个平台之争，胜出的是完整的助手体验，而非模型本身。
    AI assistants will be the next platform war, won by the complete assistant experience, not the model alone.
  • 优秀的 AI 助手需要上下文、记忆、交互、技能和反思协同工作。
    A great AI assistant requires context, memory, interaction, skills, and reflection working together.
  • 真正的护城河是围绕你长期构建的助手，而非正在商品化的模型。
    The real moat is the assistant built around you over time, not the commoditizing model.
  • 设备联邦（手机、手表、笔记本电脑、眼镜）提供比任何云服务更丰富的上下文。
    The Federation of Devices (phone, watch, laptop, glasses) provides richer context than any cloud service.
  • 本地 AI 处理日常任务，云端模型处理复杂推理，以兼顾成本和隐私。
    Local AI handles routine tasks; cloud models handle complex reasoning for cost and privacy.
  • 苹果的设备生态系统和信任度使其在控制 AI 助手平台方面占据有利地位。
    Apple's device ecosystem and trust give it a strong position to control the AI assistant platform.

局限 · Limitations

  • 本文是观点文章，非同行评审研究，缺乏实证证据。
    The article is an opinion piece, not a peer-reviewed study, lacking empirical evidence.
  • 作者对 Plumerai 的投资可能使其对本地 AI 的倡导存在偏见。
    The author's investment in Plumerai may bias his advocacy for local AI.
  • 设备联邦概念假设无缝互操作性，但尚未实现。
    The Federation of Devices concept assumes seamless interoperability, which is not yet realized.
  • 深度个性化 AI 助手的隐私和数据所有权问题未得到充分解决。
    Privacy and data ownership issues of deeply personal AI assistants are not fully addressed.
  • AI 助手可能成瘾并损害人际关系的潜力被提及但未深入探讨。
    The potential for AI assistants to be addictive and harm human connection is acknowledged but not explored.
